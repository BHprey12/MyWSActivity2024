Beru

Beru is a huge humanoid shadow ant with a light purple glow, neon eyes, sharp claws, and smoky wings. Following Jinwoo's disappearance in Solo Leveling: Ragnarok, he loses a considerable amount of his magical power and is reduced to nothing more than a floating head.

Bellion

Bellion is one of the coolest characters in Solo Leveling, tbh. He's the chief commander of the Shadow Army that serves under Sung Jinwoo, our main guy. Bellion used to be a loyal servant to the previous Shadow Monarch before Jinwoo inherited the title.

Igris

Igris has eventually gained even more powerful powers with the immense promotion of his master who became the new shadow monarch after Ashborn, now having Igris' power to skip a few rank, making him a Marshal grade shadow in an instant.
